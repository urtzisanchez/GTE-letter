Studying in Australia will give me the knowledge and recognition to be able to grow in Manuel's company (Best-Marketing) or apply to a Spanish or international company. Very simple, USA, Canada, New Zealand and Australia are the top of the pyramid for Spain, so a certificate of studies there is worth a thousand times more than any certificate in Spain, even though it is the same, why? You study it in English, so the company makes sure that you are competent in the global market. Conclusion, if you want to be different from 95% of the population, you must do different things. The effort of studying for a few years in Australia will be my reward and endorsement to form a life and a dignified retirement in my country, Spain.
